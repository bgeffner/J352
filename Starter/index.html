Some Fun Facts

  • Luka is averaging 26 points, 8 rebounds and 7 assists this season
  • He was named the 2018-19 NBA Rookie of the Year
  • Luka once scored 73 points in a single game
  • He's both a five-time NBA All-Star and five-time All-NBA member
  • Luka represented Slovenia at its inagural Olympic appearance in Tokyo, finishing fourth overall
  • Before taking his talents to the NBA, Luka played for Real Madrid. He made history by becoming the youngest player to ever win the EuroLeague MVP award
